Research Area

Genetics/Genomics: Genetic architecture and transcriptome of complex traits and disease

Noncoding RNA: Biogenesis and function of (small) noncoding transcript

Medical imaging: Segmentation of tumors and organs at risk

Disease study: Etiology and biomarker (e.g., cancer, neuronal and psychiatric disorders)

Big data analytics: Novel algorithm for massive (biological) data analysis

Artificial intelligence: Heuristic search, constraint satisfaction, and combinatorial optimization
